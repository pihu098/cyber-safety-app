A new cyber scam is spreading through WhatsApp and Telegram where users receive fake job offers promising high salaries.

Scammers ask for personal details, ID proof, and sometimes money for registration. After payment, they disappear.

⚠️ Safety Tips:
- Never trust unknown job offers
- Do not share personal information
- Avoid paying any registration fees
- Verify company details before applying

Stay alert and report such scams immediately.
